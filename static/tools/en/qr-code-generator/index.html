❓ Frequently Asked Questions

A QR code (Quick Response code) is a two-dimensional barcode that can store various data types including URLs, text, contact info, and WiFi credentials. Smartphones can scan them instantly using their built-in camera app.
Click the '⬇️ Save PNG' button after generation to download a high-quality PNG file. On mobile devices, you can also long-press the QR code image to save it directly to your gallery.
You can encode URLs, plain text, email addresses (mailto:), phone numbers (tel:), SMS, WiFi credentials (WIFI:T:WPA;S:Name;P:Password;;), vCard contacts, geo-coordinates, and more.
For print use (posters, banners), choose 512px or larger. For digital display on screens, 256px is sufficient. For small prints like business cards, 128px works. Larger sizes scan more reliably, especially from a distance.
