❓ FAQ

This calculator estimates: Federal income tax (2025 brackets), Social Security (6.2%), Medicare (1.45%), and a configurable state income tax rate. It does not include retirement contributions (401k), health insurance, or other voluntary deductions.
Single filers: 10% up to $11,925 | 12% up to $48,475 | 22% up to $103,350 | 24% up to $197,300 | 32% up to $250,525 | 35% up to $626,350 | 37% above. Married filing jointly rates apply to double those thresholds.
FICA (Federal Insurance Contributions Act) covers Social Security (6.2% on wages up to $176,100 in 2025) and Medicare (1.45% with no wage cap). Employers match these contributions.
