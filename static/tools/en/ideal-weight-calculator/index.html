Ideal body weight (IBW) is the weight range considered healthy for a given height, gender, and body frame. It varies depending on the formula used, which is why this calculator shows results from three different methods.
Using the average of all three formulas provides a reliable target range. The BMI-22 standard is the most widely referenced in modern medicine, but individual body composition (muscle vs. fat) means results can vary.
