⏰ Hourly Wage Calculator

Convert between hourly rate and monthly/annual salary. Or reverse-calculate your hourly rate from a salary.

※ US federal minimum wage: $7.25/hr. Many states have higher minimums. These are gross figures before taxes.

❓ FAQ

Monthly = Hourly × Hours/Day × Days/Week × (52 ÷ 12 ≈ 4.333 weeks). For a standard 40-hour week: Monthly = Hourly × 40 × 4.333 = Hourly × 173.33.
For a 40-hour work week: 40 hours × 52 weeks ÷ 12 months ≈ 173.33 hours per month. This is commonly rounded to 173 hours. Some calculations use 2,080 hours per year (52 × 40).
Hourly = Annual ÷ 2,080 (for a standard 40-hour week). Example: $52,000 ÷ 2,080 = $25/hr. Use the reverse calculator to get this automatically.
Under the US Fair Labor Standards Act, non-exempt employees must receive 1.5× their regular rate for hours over 40 per week. Some states require daily overtime (over 8 hours/day). Salaried exempt employees typically don't receive overtime.
