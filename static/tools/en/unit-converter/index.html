📏 Unit Converter

Convert between length, weight, temperature, area, and volume units instantly.

❓ FAQ

1 inch = 2.54 cm exactly. So 6 feet (72 inches) = 182.88 cm.
1 pound = 0.453592 kg. Conversely, 1 kg = approximately 2.205 lbs.
°C = (°F − 32) × 5/9. Example: 98.6°F = (98.6-32)×5/9 = 37°C (normal body temperature).
1 m² = 10.7639 ft². So a 100 m² apartment ≈ 1,076 sq ft.
1 US gallon = 3.78541 liters. 1 UK imperial gallon = 4.54609 liters.
