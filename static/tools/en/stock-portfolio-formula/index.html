📊 Google Sheets Stock Portfolio Formula Generator

Generate ready-to-use GOOGLEFINANCE formulas for your Google Sheets stock portfolio. Automatically calculate current value, P&L, and return % for domestic and international stocks.

How to use
① Enter your stocks: exchange, ticker, quantity, and average purchase price.
② Click [Generate Formulas] → formulas are generated automatically.
③ Click [Copy to Clipboard] → paste into Google Sheets cell A1 (Ctrl+V).

📈 Case 1: Basic Portfolio Return Tracker

The essential portfolio template. Column E (Current Price) is fetched automatically by GOOGLEFINANCE. Columns F–I auto-calculate value, cost, P&L, and return %.

Ticker(A)Name(B)Shares(C)Avg Cost(D)Price(E)Market Val(F)Cost Basis(G)P&L(H)Return%(I)
NASDAQ:AAPLApple10180=GOOGLEFINANCE(A2,"price")=C2*E2=C2*D2=F2-G2=IFERROR(H2/G2*100,0)&"%"
NASDAQ:MSFTMicrosoft8380=GOOGLEFINANCE(A3,"price")=C3*E3=C3*D3=F3-G3=IFERROR(H3/G3*100,0)&"%"
NYSE:BRK.BBerkshire B20350=GOOGLEFINANCE(A4,"price")=C4*E4=C4*D4=F4-G4=IFERROR(H4/G4*100,0)&"%"
Total=SUM(F2:F4)=SUM(G2:G4)=SUM(H2:H4)=IFERROR(SUM(H2:H4)/SUM(G2:G4)*100,0)&"%"

💱 Case 2: Multi-Currency Portfolio (USD + KRW)

Track a mixed portfolio with automatic currency conversion. =GOOGLEFINANCE("CURRENCY:USDKRW") fetches the live USD/KRW rate to convert USD positions to KRW.

Ticker(A)Shares(B)Avg Cost$(C)Price$(D)USD/KRW(E)Value₩(F)Cost₩(G)P&L₩(H)
NASDAQ:AAPL10180=GOOGLEFINANCE(A2,"price")=GOOGLEFINANCE("CURRENCY:USDKRW")=B2*D2*E2=B2*C2*E2=F2-G2
NYSE:TSLA5220=GOOGLEFINANCE(A3,"price")=GOOGLEFINANCE("CURRENCY:USDKRW")=B3*D3*E3=B3*C3*E3=F3-G3
NASDAQ:NVDA6450=GOOGLEFINANCE(A4,"price")=GOOGLEFINANCE("CURRENCY:USDKRW")=B4*D4*E4=B4*C4*E4=F4-G4
Total=SUM(F2:F4)=SUM(G2:G4)=SUM(H2:H4)

📆 Case 4: Historical Price Data

Use GOOGLEFINANCE's optional 3rd–5th arguments to pull daily or weekly historical prices into an array. Great for charting or backtesting returns.

FormulaDescription
=GOOGLEFINANCE("AAPL","price",DATE(2024,1,1),DATE(2024,12,31),"DAILY")Apple daily close prices for 2024
=GOOGLEFINANCE("AAPL","price",DATE(2024,1,1),30)Apple prices for 30 days from 2024-01-01
=GOOGLEFINANCE("AAPL","price",DATE(2024,1,1),DATE(2024,12,31),"WEEKLY")Apple weekly prices for 2024
=GOOGLEFINANCE("CURRENCY:USDKRW","price",DATE(2024,1,1),DATE(2024,12,31),"DAILY")Daily USD/KRW exchange rate history for 2024
=GOOGLEFINANCE("AAPL","all",DATE(2024,6,1),DATE(2024,6,30),"DAILY")Apple OHLCV data for June 2024
