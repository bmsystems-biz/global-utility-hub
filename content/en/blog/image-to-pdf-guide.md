## Why Convert Images to PDF?

Images work great for photos, but PDF is the preferred format for:

- **Official document submission**: Forms, receipts, contracts look more professional as PDF
- **Combining multiple files**: Bundle several images into one file for easy sharing
- **Universal compatibility**: PDF looks identical on every device and OS
- **Print quality**: PDFs scale well to any print size

## Supported Image Formats

The Image to PDF Converter supports any image format your browser can display:
- JPG / JPEG
- PNG
- WebP
- GIF
- BMP

## Step-by-Step Guide

1. **Upload images**: Click the upload zone or drag and drop files directly
2. **Check order**: Multiple images become separate pages in the listed order
3. **Set orientation**: Auto (based on image shape), Portrait, or Landscape
4. **Set margin**: None, Small (10mm), or Standard (20mm)
5. **Click Convert**: The PDF downloads automatically when complete

## Privacy First

Many online PDF converters upload your files to cloud servers. This is a problem for sensitive documents like:
- Tax returns and bank statements
- Medical records
- ID cards and passports
- Signed contracts

The Global Utility Hub Image to PDF Converter converts everything locally in your browser. Your files never touch any server.

## When to Use Each Page Orientation

- **Auto**: Best for mixed-content batches (some portrait, some landscape images)
- **Portrait**: Standard documents, scanned papers, vertical photos
- **Landscape**: Screenshots, wide charts, horizontal photos

## Margin Recommendations

- **No margin**: Maximum image size on page — good for photos
- **10mm (Small)**: Slight breathing room, most common setting
- **20mm (Standard)**: Formal documents, reports, official submissions
