## Why Count Days to Your Birthday?

Knowing the exact countdown to a birthday — yours or someone else's — lets you plan celebrations, prepare gifts, or simply satisfy curiosity. The Korean Age Calculator shows this instantly alongside your current age.

## Fun Age Milestones Worth Celebrating

### Day-Based Milestones
- **1,000th day of life**: Around age 2 years, 8 months — a popular celebration in Korea (돌잔치 at 100 days, then 1,000 days)
- **10,000th day**: Approximately age 27 years and 5 months
- **Your 100th month birthday**: Every 8 years and 4 months

### Year-Based Milestones
- **60th birthday (환갑/Hwangap)**: In East Asian tradition, completing one full 60-year cycle of the Chinese zodiac is a major celebration
- **70th birthday (고희)**: A milestone referenced in classical Chinese poetry

## Legal Age Thresholds in South Korea

After the 2023 Age Unification Act, South Korea uses **만나이** (international age) for all legal purposes:

| Activity | Age (만나이) |
|----------|--------------|
| Voting | 18 |
| Drinking/Smoking | 19 |
| Military service | 18–28 (varies) |
| Driver's license | 18 |

If your birthday hasn't passed this year, you'll be 1 year younger than your birth year suggests — which matters for borderline cases.
